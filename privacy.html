Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Picanik (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate our pre-launch platform:

  • Email address — when you sign up for early access updates
  • Role selection — such as mod developer, server owner, or player
  • Optional notes — information you choose to share with us

We do not require account creation at this stage and do not collect payment information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send early access and launch-related updates
  • Understand the needs of the Hytale modding community
  • Invite users to relevant programs or discussions
  • Improve our platform planning and priorities

We do not sell your personal information and do not use it for unrelated marketing.

Analytics

We may use basic analytics tools to understand general site usage (such as page visits or referral sources). These tools do not identify you personally.

Discord and External Links

Our site includes links to external services such as Discord. Interactions with those services are governed by their own privacy policies.

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you provide. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

You may unsubscribe from our emails at any time using the link included in each message, or by contacting us directly.

Children’s Privacy

Picanik is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform evolves. Any changes will be posted on this page.
