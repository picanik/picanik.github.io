Why Picanik exists

Every modding community starts fragmented — Discord uploads, GitHub zips, forum posts, and temporary links.

Picanik exists to give Hytale a real mod foundation from day one: a place where mods are hosted, versions are tracked, and creators and server owners aren’t forced to build their own infrastructure.

What Picanik is not

  • Not a forum
  • Not a news site
  • Not a link aggregator
  • Not an in-game marketplace replacement

Picanik is external infrastructure — built to serve the ecosystem, not control it.

Early access is limited

We’re opening Picanik gradually with a small group of mod developers and server owners to shape the platform before public launch.

  • Founding Creator roles
  • Early publishing access
  • Direct influence on features
  • Visibility when the ecosystem forms
